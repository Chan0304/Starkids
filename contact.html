Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

+

We offer a comprehensive range of spiritual services including guided meditation sessions, energy healing, chakra balancing, spiritual counseling, consciousness exploration workshops, and cosmic wisdom teachings. Each service is tailored to your individual needs and spiritual journey.

Do I need prior experience with meditation?

+

No prior experience is necessary! We welcome individuals at all levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Our sessions are designed to meet you where you are on your spiritual journey.

How long are the sessions?

+

Session durations vary depending on the service and your needs. Meditation sessions typically range from 30 minutes to 2 hours, while energy healing sessions are usually 60-90 minutes. We can discuss the best duration for your specific needs.

What should I expect from my first session?

+

Your first session will begin with a consultation to understand your goals and current spiritual practice. We'll discuss your intentions and create a personalized approach. The session itself will be gentle and guided, ensuring you feel comfortable and supported throughout.

Do you offer group sessions or workshops?

+

Yes! We regularly host group meditation sessions, workshops, and spiritual retreats. These events provide opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals and deepen your practice in a supportive community environment.

How can I prepare for a session?

+

Come with an open mind and heart. Wear comfortable clothing, avoid heavy meals before the session, and arrive a few minutes early to settle in. Most importantly, bring your authentic self - no special preparation is needed beyond your willingness to explore.
