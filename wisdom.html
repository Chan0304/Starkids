Galaxy Wisdom Cards

Consciousness

You are not a drop in the ocean. You are the entire ocean in a drop. Your consciousness is infinite and eternal, connected to all that exists.

"The mind is everything. What you think you become."

Energy

Everything is energy and that's all there is to it. Match the frequency of the reality you want and you cannot help but get that reality.

"Energy flows where attention goes."

Presence

The present moment is filled with joy and happiness. If you are attentive, you will see it. Life is available only in the present moment.

"Peace comes from within. Do not seek it without."

Transformation

The wound is the place where the Light enters you. Every experience, even the difficult ones, are opportunities for growth and transformation.

"What you are is what you have been, and what you will be is what you do now."

Meditation

The greatest meditation is a mind that lets go. When you let go of all thoughts and concepts, you experience the natural state of awareness.

"The mind is like water. When it's turbulent, it's difficult to see. When it's peaceful, everything becomes clear."

Happiness

Happiness is not something ready-made. It comes from your own actions. True happiness arises from within, independent of external circumstances.

"You yourself, as much as anybody in the entire universe, deserve your love and affection."

Unity

The universe is not outside of you. Look inside yourself; everything that you want, you already are. We are all one consciousness.

"You are the universe experiencing itself."

Love

Love is the most powerful force in the universe. When you love yourself and others unconditionally, you align with the highest vibration of existence.

"Love is the bridge between you and everything."

Freedom

True freedom comes from understanding that you are not your thoughts, emotions, or circumstances. You are the awareness that observes them.

"Freedom is not the absence of commitments, but the ability to choose what you are committed to."
